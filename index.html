About MX Offroad Master

MX Offroad Master is the premier online motorcycle racing game that combines realistic physics with arcade-style fun. Designed for both casual gamers and motorcycle enthusiasts, this free-to-play browser game offers hours of entertainment without requiring any downloads or installations.

With intuitive controls that are easy to learn but difficult to master, players of all skill levels can immediately jump into the action. As you progress through increasingly challenging tracks, you'll unlock new motorcycles, customize your rider, and compete for the fastest times on global leaderboards.

Game Controls

Keyboard

  • W / Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S / Down Arrow: Brake
  • A / Left Arrow: Lean backward
  • D / Right Arrow: Lean forward
  • Space: Perform tricks (when airborne)
  • R: Restart level
